Welcome to Computer Engineering

Computer Engineering is a dynamic and fast-growing field that combines principles of computer science and electrical engineering. It focuses on the design, development, and implementation of computer hardware and software systems.

The discipline covers a wide range of topics such as artificial intelligence, data science, cybersecurity, embedded systems, and computer networks. With technological advancements, Computer Engineering has become essential in virtually every industry.

At the School of Computer Engineering, we provide world-class education and training to prepare students for the challenges of a technology-driven world. Our curriculum emphasizes innovation, problem-solving, and practical applications to shape the future of technology.
